Bridging ancient wisdom with modern technology

As a Cultural Navigator, I bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and cutting-edge technology. My unique multicultural background—spanning Thai heritage, Japanese influences, and British education—provides me with a distinctive perspective on global innovation.

I specialize in AI development and cross-cultural design, creating solutions that honor traditional values while embracing technological advancement. My work focuses on making technology more inclusive, culturally sensitive, and globally accessible.

Through my journey across cultures, I've learned that the most powerful innovations emerge when we combine diverse perspectives with modern capabilities. I'm passionate about creating technology that serves humanity while respecting our rich cultural heritage.
