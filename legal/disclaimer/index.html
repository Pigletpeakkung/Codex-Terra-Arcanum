1. General Disclaimer

The information on our websites (pegearts.com, portfolio site, and related platforms) is provided for general informational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy and cultural sensitivity, we make no representations or warranties about the completeness, accuracy, or reliability of any information.

Our multicultural perspective informs our work, but individual cultural contexts may require specific considerations not covered in our general content. Every cultural situation is unique and may need tailored approaches.

Important Notice

This disclaimer does not limit our commitment to cultural respect, ethical conduct, or professional standards. Our obligations regarding cultural heritage protection and community rights remain fully in effect.

3. Cultural Sensitivity

Cultural Representation

  • We represent our own multicultural experience and perspective
  • Cannot speak for all cultural communities or traditions
  • Encourage direct engagement with relevant cultural communities
  • Facilitate rather than replace authentic cultural voices
  • Acknowledge the limitations of any individual perspective

Traditional Knowledge

  • Belongs to originating communities and knowledge holders
  • Shared only with appropriate permissions and protocols
  • Subject to cultural laws and traditional governance
  • Protected under indigenous rights and cultural sovereignty
  • May have restrictions on use, sharing, or modification

Cross-Cultural Work

  • Requires ongoing learning and cultural humility
  • May involve misunderstandings despite best intentions
  • Committed to respectful correction and improvement
  • Values community feedback and guidance
  • Recognizes that cultural competence is a journey, not a destination

Our Cultural Commitment

While we cannot claim perfect cultural knowledge, we commit to approaching all cultural matters with respect, humility, and a willingness to learn. We prioritize community voices and traditional knowledge holders in all cultural heritage work.
